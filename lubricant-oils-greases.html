Range of Products

Gear Oils

High-performance oils specially engineered to ensure smooth and efficient gear transmission, even under extreme pressure, high loads, and fluctuating temperatures.

Hydraulic Oils

Engineered to deliver reliable and efficient performance in a wide range of hydraulic systems, these oils offer excellent protection against wear, corrosion, and oxidation.

Machinery Oils

Specifically formulated to eliminate stick-slip issues and ensure smooth, precise movement of sliding surfaces in machine tools. These oils create a strong lubricating film

Machine Tool Way Oils

Specially developed to reduce stick-slip and ensure smooth, controlled movement on the sliding surfaces of machine tools. These oils form a strong, adhesive lubricating film that resists being wiped away under high pressures

Cutting & Honing Oils

Specialized oils used during metal cutting, grinding, and honing operations to improve finish and reduce tool wear.
